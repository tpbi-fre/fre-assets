## 6. CREATIVE DIRECTION

### Visual Concept: "Sharp."
Peppermint isn't just a flavor — it's a feeling. That first hit of cold clarity. The moment your mind snaps into focus. Every visual should evoke sharpness: clean lines, high contrast, precise typography.

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Deep Navy (Pantone 281C) — the Peppermint color
- Secondary: FRE Red (#E40032) — brand anchor
- Accent: Ice white, silver metallic (for LTO premium feel)
- Background: Charcoal/black for drama, white for retail clarity

**Photography/video direction:**
- High contrast, dramatic lighting
- Close-up texture shots (frost, metal, condensation — the "cold" feeling)
- Fighter imagery: shadow/silhouette, not posed hero shots
- Product shots: tin at angle, always hero-lit, never flat catalog style

**LTO tin concept guidance:**
- Deep navy base (matte finish)
- "250" mark integrated, not slapped on
- UFC partnership lockup (secondary, not competing with FRE)
- Limited-edition numbering on bottom or inner lid
- Premium unboxing feel — this is a collector's piece
