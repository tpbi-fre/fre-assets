# on! PLUS Competitive Threat Brief
**Prepared by:** Vera ⚡ | **Date:** March 27, 2026
**Source:** FDA press release, Altria IR, Tobacco Insider, Tobacco Journal International, Seeking Alpha, Reuters
**Brand scope:** on! PLUS (Helix/Altria) vs FRE (TPB)
**Time peg:** March 2026 nationwide launch

---

## TL;DR

Altria's on! PLUS hit nationwide retail on March 23, 2026. It's the **first nicotine pouch authorized through FDA's expedited PMTA pilot program** — a regulatory moat FRE doesn't have yet. Limited portfolio (6 SKUs), premium positioning, and Altria's distribution muscle make this a serious competitive entry. FRE's advantages: deeper strength range, more flavors, faster growth trajectory, and price positioning. But the FDA stamp changes the game at retail.

## What Makes on! PLUS Dangerous

### 1. FDA Authorization = Retail Leverage
on! PLUS is the **only nicotine pouch with FDA marketing authorization through the pilot PMTA program** (ZYN's 15 SKUs were authorized separately in Jan 2025). Retailers now have two "FDA-blessed" pouch brands to point to. Category buyers at major chains will increasingly ask: *"Is this FDA-authorized?"* — and FRE's answer is still "pending."

### 2. Altria's Distribution Machine
Altria owns the DSD (direct store delivery) network that services ~250,000+ US retail locations. Wholesale deliveries started Mar 16; retail hit Mar 23. They can achieve near-total distribution in weeks, not months. FRE is still building chain-by-chain.

### 3. Positioned as "Next-Gen"
NICOSLIK technology, soft-feel pouch material, built-in disposal compartment — Altria is framing on! PLUS as a premium innovation play, not just another pouch. This is a consumer experience story, not just nicotine delivery.

### 4. First-Mover in FDA Pilot Track
The pilot program is designed to expedite PMTA reviews for nicotine pouch products. on! PLUS going first means FDA has established review precedent — this could speed up or complicate FRE's own review depending on how standards evolve.

---

## FRE's Competitive Advantages

### 1. Portfolio Depth
FRE offers **5x the strength range** (3–15mg vs 6–9mg) and **2x the flavor selection**. High-strength users (12mg, 15mg) have no on! PLUS option. FRE Mint 15mg is the top MSA SKU for a reason — strength seekers are loyal.

### 2. Growth Trajectory
FRE run rate: ~$125–130M projected with ~628% YoY growth. on! (legacy) has been losing share — on! PLUS is Altria's attempt to reverse that with a better product. FRE is growing into Altria's declining position.

### 3. Synthetic Nicotine Tax Arbitrage
FRE uses synthetic nicotine → $0 excise tax in ~35–40 states. on! PLUS uses tobacco-derived nicotine → subject to state excise taxes. This gives FRE a margin and pricing advantage in most markets.

### 4. Brand Authenticity
FRE's positioning ("Own Your Edge," Walton Goggins voice) is authentic and resonates with adult professionals. on! PLUS has Altria's corporate machinery — effective but impersonal. FRE can outmaneuver on voice.

### 5. Peppermint LTO Timing
FRE's Peppermint LTO (July 4 drop) gives a near-term differentiation moment. on! PLUS has exactly zero limited editions or flavor innovation in pipeline.

## Key Unknowns

1. **on! PLUS 12mg** — Altria submitted for 12mg authorization too; FDA decision pending. If approved, strength gap narrows.
2. **Retail pricing strategy** — Altria may use trade funding to undercut on price in key chains.
3. **Planogram deals** — Are Altria reps already negotiating shelf placement (à la EG America + RJR/VELO deal)?
4. **FRE PMTA timeline** — Any signal from FDA on review progress? This is the single biggest strategic question.

---

## Bottom Line

on! PLUS is the most credible new competitive threat FRE has faced. FDA authorization + Altria distribution + premium positioning is a potent combination. But it's launching with **a thin portfolio** (6 SKUs) into a market where FRE has **5x the strength range, 2x the flavors, explosive growth, and a tax advantage.** The real risk isn't today — it's if/when Altria gets the 12mg authorized and starts winning planogram wars. FRE's play: **velocity, strength depth, and speed to market** (Peppermint, Mega Pack, vending). Move faster than Altria's bureaucracy.
