- **20 pouches per tin** (vs. ZYN's 15). 33% more product, same price point.
- **100-count Mega Packs** for people who don't want to think about it for a month.
- **No tobacco. No spit. No smoke. No apology.**
- **"Own Your Edge"** — FRE is a precision tool for people who have shit to do.

### Gym Activation Playbook

**The Offer (What Gyms Get):**
- Free FRE floor mat for the gym (octagon-shaped — callback to our earlier conversation)
- Branded counter display at front desk with sample tins
- Monthly Mega Pack allocation for gym owners/coaches (make THEM users first)
- Co-branded "Roll With FRE" or "Train With FRE" signage
- Optional: FRE-branded rashguards for the gym's competition team

**The Ask (What FRE Gets):**
- Floor mat displayed in main training area
- Counter display at point of sale
- Permission to sample at open mats / fight nights
- Social media post from gym account (1x/month minimum)
- Logo on gym's website sponsor page

**Activation Tactics:**

1. **"Roll With FRE" Open Mat Series**
   - Monthly FRE-sponsored open mat at partner gyms
   - Free entry, free FRE samples, light branding
   - Creates recurring touchpoint without being obnoxious
   - Film 60-second recap clips for social

2. **Coach Ambassador Program**
   - Gym owners and head coaches are the most trusted voices in any gym
   - Supply them personally — make them believers first
   - If the coach uses it, the gym follows. That's how trust-based communities work.

3. **Competition Sponsorship**
   - Sponsor local/regional BJJ and MMA competitions in Tier 1 cities
   - Banner placement, sampling at weigh-ins, branded warm-up areas
   - "FRE Submission of the Night" award at local MMA events — $500 bonus to the winner

4. **"Gym vs. Gym" Content Series**
   - Pit two FRE-sponsored gyms against each other in friendly competition
   - Film it. Post it. Let the communities drive engagement.
   - Builds tribal loyalty without FRE having to create the tribe

5. **Fighter Pipeline**
   - Use gym partnerships to identify up-and-coming fighters for FRE ambassador deals
   - Sign them EARLY (cheap) and grow with them
   - When they make it to UFC, the FRE relationship is already authentic

# CROSS-PARTNERSHIP ACTIVATION IDEAS

These concepts work across multiple properties simultaneously:

### 1. "Own Your Edge" Strength Finder
Interactive digital experience (web + social) where users answer questions about their lifestyle/intensity level and get matched to their FRE strength. Hooks into every partnership:
- "You train like a UFC fighter → 15mg"
- "You grind Chess.com rated games til 2AM → 6mg"
- "You ride bulls for a living → you already know"
Drive to purchase with a trial offer.

### 2. "The FRE Roster"
Unified ambassador program across all partnerships. 20-30 athletes/creators/personalities spanning UFC, BJJ, WSM, PBR, 100T, and chess. They're all "on the roster." Creates a sense of scale — FRE isn't in one sport, FRE is in every arena where performance matters.

### 3. "Different Arena, Same Edge"
Campaign concept: Split-screen content showing radically different FRE moments.
- Left: UFC fighter in the tunnel. Right: Chess grandmaster at the board.
- Left: Bull rider in the chute. Right: 100T player loading into ranked.
- Left: Strongman gripping an atlas stone. Right: Formula Drift driver gripping the wheel.
Same pouch. Same edge. Different arena.
This is the brand campaign that ties everything together.

### 4. Mega Pack Subscription + Partnership Perks
Launch a FRE subscription (Mega Pack monthly). Subscribers get rotating partnership perks:
- Month 1: Chess.com Diamond
- Month 2: 100T merch discount
- Month 3: PBR event ticket raffle
- Month 4: WSM viewing party invite
Cross-pollination between fanbases. Every subscriber discovers a new FRE world.

### 5. "No Spit Zone" Tour
Traveling activation that hits events across ALL TKO properties + 100T events. Same core setup (sampling, displays, interactive experiences) but themed to each property. Creates a consistent FRE presence across the entire TKO calendar.

### 6. Limited Edition Collab Tins
One per partnership, per year:
- UFC: Black tin, Octagon emboss, 15mg
- Formula Drift: Checkered flag design, 12mg
- WSM: Oversized "Mega Tin" (100-count), 15mg — "The Strongest Tin"
- PBR: Western tooled leather design, 9mg
- 100 Thieves: Streetwear drop aesthetic, co-branded, 6mg
- Chess.com: Marble/wood chess aesthetic, 6mg
Collectible. Each one drives trial in a new audience. Limited runs create urgency.
