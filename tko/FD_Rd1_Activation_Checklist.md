## THE OPPORTUNITY

Formula Drift Rd 1 is FRE's backyard event. Long Beach is 30 minutes from LA. This is the **first FD event under the TKO deal** — sets the tone for 7 more rounds through October.

The crowd: 18-35, overwhelmingly male, gearheads and lifestyle brand enthusiasts. Peak pouch demo. And unlike UFC (broadcast-heavy), FD is a **physical, boots-on-ground activation** — sampling, face-to-face brand experience.
