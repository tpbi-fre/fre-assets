## CREATIVE DIRECTION

### Brand Elements (Mandatory)

| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| **Logo** | FRE wordmark — always capitalized, never "Fre" or "fre" |
| **Primary Color** | FRE Red `#E40032` (Pantone 185C) |
| **Typography** | Helvetica Neue (Bold for headlines, Regular for body) |
| **Tagline** | "Own Your Edge" |
| **Partner Lockup** | "Official Partner of Formula Drift" — per TKO deal designation |
| **21+ Disclaimer** | Required on all consumer-facing assets |

### Visual Direction

| Principle | Application |
|---|---|
| **Bold, not busy** | Track signage is seen at speed. Big logo, clear contrast, minimal text. |
| **FRE Red dominant** | Red on dark (black/charcoal `#333333`) or red on white. Maximum visibility against outdoor backdrop. |
| **Action energy** | FD audience is automotive/lifestyle. Think drift smoke, speed lines, industrial edge — NOT corporate clean. |
| **"The Edge" aesthetic** | Sharp angles, bold typography, the sense that FRE is for people who push limits. |

### Hierarchy (what to emphasize, in order)

1. **FRE Logo** — biggest element, unmistakable at 50+ feet
2. **"Own Your Edge"** — tagline beneath logo
3. **"Official Partner of Formula Drift"** — partner designation
4. **Product shot** (optional on larger assets like billboards) — FRE can with visible flavor branding
5. **21+ disclaimer** — required but can be small/bottom
