🚨 The Texas Anomaly

Texas accounts for 20.2% of FRE's national volume from just 11.7% of stores. At 76.1 units/store, Texas velocity is 1.9× the rest-of-US average (39.7). Texas produces ~116,700 excess units per 4-week period above its "fair share" — that's 9.7% of total national volume that exists purely because Texas sells faster, not because it has more doors. FRE's 8.8% category share in Texas is the highest of any state — nearly 4× the national blended share. If every state sold like Texas, FRE would be a ~2.1M unit/month brand instead of ~1.2M.
