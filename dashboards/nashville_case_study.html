🏆 THE PLAYBOOK 🏆
Strategic Framework
THE NASHVILLE FORMULA
Three steps. Every market. Repeatable.
01

FIND THE ANCHOR

Every market has a Twice Daily. Tri-Star = 59 doors, 89% pen, 45,107 vol, +32% acceleration. One chain partnership generates 82% of chain-level FRE volume in the entire metro.

02

LAYER ITP

87 enrolled independents at 5.38% SOC — double the 2.48% non-enrolled. Active ratio 79% vs 63%. Power Set 90% vs 37%. $441K net/year on $183K blended spend. 242% ROI.

03

SPIN THE FLYWHEEL

Events and DTC amplify, they don't start it. Nashville has 459,630 DTC units — 2.7× the 172,219 retail volume. Every DTC order in a ZIP without a retail store = a door you should open.

THE CFO PITCH

"We spent $183K on ITP in Nashville and generated $624K in incremental revenue — 242% ROI. Enrolled stores have 5.38% SOC vs 2.48% non-enrolled. Alt 16 specifically generates 566% ROI at $250/mo. We want to replicate this in Austin, Chicago, and 10 more metros."
